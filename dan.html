3 Important — Check Your Windows Version

For your secretary to be able to connect remotely to your PC, the computer needs to be running Windows 11 Pro — not the standard Windows 11 Home edition. Pro has a built-in feature called "Remote Desktop" that Home doesn't, and we'll be using it to let her log in from her house.

!

What if I don't have Windows 11 Pro?

No problem at all. You can upgrade from Home to Pro right inside Windows for $99, paid once to Microsoft. No new computer, no reinstall — your apps, files, and settings all stay exactly as they are. I'll handle the upgrade for you as part of the setup if needed.

How to check which version you have (takes 20 seconds):
  1. Click the Start button (Windows icon, bottom-left of your screen)
  2. Type About your PC and press Enter
  3. Scroll down to the "Windows specifications" section
  4. Look at the "Edition" line — it'll say either Windows 11 Home or Windows 11 Pro
