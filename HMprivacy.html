HandyMan Privacy Policy

Job & Invoice Pro

Effective Date: March 6, 2026

Overview

HandyMan (“the App”) is developed by Daniel Pellegrini, operating as Oahu App Design. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that data.

The short version: Your data stays on your devices and in your private iCloud account. We never see it, collect it, or store it on our servers.

Information We Do Not Collect

HandyMan does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Business data (customers, invoices, quotes, jobs)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Data Storage & iCloud

All data you create in HandyMan — customers, invoices, quotes, jobs, services, and company settings — is stored in two places:

  • On your device — for offline access and fast performance.
  • In your private iCloud account — using Apple’s CloudKit framework for syncing across your devices.

Your iCloud data is protected by your Apple ID credentials and Apple’s security infrastructure. HandyMan uses only your private CloudKit database, meaning no other user or the developer can access your records.

For more information on how Apple protects your iCloud data, visit Apple’s iCloud security overview.

Third-Party Services

HandyMan does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs beyond Apple’s own frameworks:

  • CloudKit — for iCloud data sync (Apple)
  • PDFKit / UIKit — for generating invoice and report PDFs locally on your device
  • StoreKit — for App Store purchase processing (Apple)

No data is sent to any server outside of Apple’s iCloud infrastructure.

Data Sharing

HandyMan allows you to share invoices and reports as PDF files via the iOS share sheet (email, text, AirDrop, etc.). When you share a document, it is sent directly from your device to the recipient you choose. The developer has no access to or visibility into shared documents.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party.

Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored in your private iCloud account:

  • Your data exists as long as you keep it in the App.
  • Deleting the App removes local data from that device.
  • Data in iCloud persists until you delete it (via the App’s built-in tools or through iCloud settings).
  • You can erase all App data at any time using the Erase All Data feature in the App’s Setup screen.

We have no ability to access or delete your data because it is stored entirely in your private iCloud container.

Children’s Privacy

HandyMan is a business tool intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The App is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content.

Security

Your data security is handled by Apple’s iCloud infrastructure, which includes:

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit
  • AES-128 or AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Authentication via your Apple ID

The App itself does not implement additional encryption because Apple’s CloudKit private database already provides robust security by design.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
